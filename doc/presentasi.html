1
Create Delivery
Pengelola ships cassette to RC with courier details & tracking
2
Receive at RC
RC Staff confirms receipt ‚Üí Status: RECEIVED, Cassette: IN_REPAIR
3
Create Repair Ticket
RC Staff creates repair ticket and assigns to engineer
4
Process Repair
Engineer: DIAGNOSING ‚Üí ON_PROGRESS ‚Üí COMPLETED
5
Quality Control
QC Pass ‚Üí READY_FOR_PICKUP | QC Fail ‚Üí SCRAPPED
6
Pickup Confirmation
RC confirms pickup with signature ‚Üí Ticket CLOSED, Cassette OK

Ticket Status

Cassette Status

Status Deskripsi Trigger Action
OK Operational, siap digunakan Pickup confirmed / Initial state
BAD Faulty, perlu repair Ticket created
IN_TRANSIT_TO_RC Sedang dikirim ke RC Delivery created
IN_REPAIR Sedang diperbaiki di RC Received at RC
READY_FOR_PICKUP Perbaikan selesai, siap diambil QC passed
SCRAPPED Tidak bisa diperbaiki, butuh replacement QC failed / Beyond repair
